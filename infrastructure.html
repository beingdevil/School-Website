--LIBRARY--

READ Program-Books are the most faithful friends in life.As a part of our various initiatives, we have embarked upon the READ program for classes I-XI in the current academic session. This program has now been made mandatory by CBSE. This innovative approach will help children develop reading habits, literary and language skills, moral values and other cognitive skills. This programme, helps our children enjoy reading and nurture their young minds along with subtly enhancing their language skills while making them good human beings. To make this programme more effective, the school has also set up a very large library which is easily accessible to both the students and the staff
