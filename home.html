--INTRODUCTION--
Jai Academy was established in 2002 to perpetuate the memory of Late Shri Jai Chandra Arya, the great pillar on which the foundation of the present Jagaran Group family stands. "Danik Jagaran" is the No.1 newspaper in hindi with largest readership in North India having its birth way back in 1942 in this historical place, Jhansi. To commemorate its Platinum Jubilee Year and make the dream come true of the eminent philanthrophist, late shri Jai Chand Arya, "Danik Jagran" dedicated to its birth place an institution to the inculcation of humanitarian values and high educational standards.

Jai Academy is an opening of a new era in a school education in Jhansi. It is not just another school - ''It's a school with a difference''. In tune with the reputation of its promoters, the school holds an assurance for providing educational facilities of the highest order and also ensures fullest development in the field of academics, co-curricular activities and character building of the field children entrusted to it. The institution has made rapid strides and now occupies a place of pride in the city.

The school is an english medium and presently up to class XII Affiliated to C.B.S.E New Delhi.
